Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is this all automated?
Yes, mostly. Gekko's Birthday uses an agentic LLM sourcing system to collect, score, and filter the latest news on the web and deliver it to Mr. Gekko's desk each morning. The insights are all his. The final newsletter is then published and promoted automatically using a network of cloud microservices.
2. What are the MBA Links?
These are free online resources that match many required and supplementary readings for the MBA Program at the University of California Irvine, Paul Merage Business School where I am a student.
3. What are the Whitepapers?
These are my original research papers on a variety of topics related to business, technology, and culture. They are available for free download on my Papers Page.
4. What was that movie?
The inspiration for this page is the iconic scene from Wall Street where Bud Fox (Charlie Sheen) wakes up to the alarm on his 1985 Apple Home PC reminding him that May 6, 1985 was Gordon Gekko's birthday. Box of cigars in hand, he worms his way into Michael Douglas's office and launches the relationship that changes his life.
5. Haven't I seen you before?
